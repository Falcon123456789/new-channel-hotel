About New Channel Inn Hotel
Welcome to New Channel Inn Hotel Goroka

Where Heritage Meets Comfort

Welcome to New Channel Inn Hotel Located in the heart of Goroka, Eastern Highlands Province, New Channel Inn Hotel offers comfortable accommodation for business and leisure travellers. Conveniently located only minutes from Goroka Airport, the hotel provides modern rooms, complimentary Wi-Fi, secure parking, a restaurant and bar, and warm Papua New Guinean hospitality. Whether you are visiting for work, tourism, or the famous Goroka Show, New Channel Inn Hotel is your ideal place to stay.
