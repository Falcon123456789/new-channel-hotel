Our Services

In Room Dining

Our in-room dining service brings a curated menu of exquisite dishes right to your door, crafted to satisfy every craving. Whether it's a hearty breakfast, a light snack, or a gourmet dinner, enjoy restaurant-quality cuisine at your convenience, anytime you desire.

Pickup & Drop Off

Whether you're heading to your destination or returning home, we ensure a smooth and comfortable journey, tailored to your schedule. Let us take the stress out of transportation so you can focus on what matters most

ATM Machine

The Kina Bank ATM facility provides convenient and accessible banking services on-site. Located in a secure and easily accessible area, the ATM offers a hassle-free banking experience for both locals and international visitors.

Busniess Centre

The Business Centre is a fully equipped space designed to cater to the needs of corporate guests and professionals. Offering a range of services such as high-speed internet, printing, faxing, and photocopying, it ensures that all your business requirements are met efficiently

The in-house laundry service offers convenient and efficient cleaning for your clothing and personal items. With a range of laundry options available, you can have your clothes professionally cleaned and returned promptly.
