Rapala - The Restaurant

Rapala offers a sophisticated dining experience with dim lighting, creating a warm and intimate atmosphere for a relaxing meal. Enjoy your dinner, accompanied by a glass of wine and soft, ambient music, as you indulge in exquisite dishes. The restaurant can accommodate up to 40 guests, making it an ideal setting for both small gatherings and special occasions. Perfect for a romantic evening, Rapala is the ultimate destination for a candlelight dinner with your special someone.

  • Ambiance

    Elegant candlelit setting with warm lighting and soft music.

  • Occasions

    Ideal for romantic dinners, intimate gatherings, and celebrations.

  • Cuisine

    Exquisite gourmet dishes with curated wine pairings.

  • Capacity

    Comfortable seating for up to 46 guests.

The Cafe

Café 247 offers a convenient and vibrant dining experience, available around the clock for guests to enjoy at any time of day or night.
